About YouTube Comment Sentiment Analyzer

  Welcome to the YouTube Comment Sentiment Analyzer! Our web application utilizes YouTube API to extract comments from the video based on the user's input, and then a powerful LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) model trained on the IMDb dataset to analyze the sentiment of YouTube comments and classify them as either positive or negative.

About Developer

  I'm a Computer Science student passionate about natural language processing and sentiment analysis. My goal is to provide accurate sentiment analysis of YouTube comments to help users understand the sentiment landscape of videos and foster meaningful discussions.

I'm continuously working to improve the accuracy and performance of our sentiment analysis model, leveraging cutting-edge techniques and incorporating user feedback.
