How it Works

  1. Data Collection: We utilize the YouTube API to extract comments from the video based on the user's input. These comments serve as the input data for our sentiment analysis.
  2. Preprocessing: The collected comments undergo preprocessing to remove noise, including special characters, numbers, and punctuation. We also perform tokenization and word embedding to represent the comments in a format suitable for the LSTM model.
  3. LSTM Model Training: Our LSTM model has been trained on a large dataset of movie reviews from IMDb, enabling it to understand the underlying sentiment patterns in text. The model has learned to distinguish between positive and negative sentiments based on the training data.
  4. Sentiment Analysis: When a user submits YouTube comments through our web application, the LSTM model predicts the sentiment associated with each comment. The model evaluates the text and assigns a sentiment label of either positive or negative.
  5. Visualize Sentiment Analysis Results: The sentiment analysis results are presented to the user through an intuitive and user-friendly interface. You can explore the sentiment distribution of the comments and gain insights into the overall sentiment of the YouTube video.

Benefits and Applications

  • Content Creators: YouTube creators can gain valuable insights into the sentiment of their audience by analyzing the comments. This allows them to understand how their content is received and make informed decisions to improve engagement.
  • Brand Reputation Management: Companies and brands can monitor the sentiment of YouTube comments related to their products or services. This information helps them understand customer sentiment, address concerns, and manage their online reputation effectively.
  • User Engagement: Users can interact with the sentiment analysis results to explore the sentiment distribution of the comments. They can also discover trending videos with positive sentiments and engage with a community of like-minded individuals.
